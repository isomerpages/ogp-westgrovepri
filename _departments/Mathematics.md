<p><strong>INTRODUCTION</strong>
</p>
<p>Mathematics is the study of patterns and relationships, and it plays a
vital role in developing students’ intellectual competencies such as logical
reasoning, spatial visualisation, analysis, and abstract thinking. Through
the learning and application of mathematical concepts, students build strong
numeracy, reasoning, and problem-solving skills.</p>
<p>At West Grove, we strive to provide a rich variety of learning experiences
that enable students to deepen their understanding of mathematical concepts
and apply them meaningfully to a wide range of real-world situations.</p>
<p><strong>MISSION / VISION</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To provide students with concrete and meaningful learning experiences
to deepen their understanding of mathematical concepts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To empower students to reason mathematically and communicate their thinking
clearly.</p>
</li>
</ul>
