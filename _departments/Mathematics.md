**INTRODUCTION**

Mathematics is the study of patterns and relationships. It is an excellent tool for the development of oneâ€™s intellectual competence in logical reasoning, spatial visualization, analysis and abstract thinking. Pupils develop numeracy, reasoning, thinking skills and problem-solving skills through learning and application of mathematics concepts taught in class.

In West Grove, we aim to provide a variety of learning experiences to help pupils develop and explore Mathematics concepts and apply them in a wide range of real-world problems.

**MISSION / VISION**

* To provide our students with concrete experiences to learn Mathematics concepts meaningfully.
* To inculcate our students in mathematical reasoning and communication.
