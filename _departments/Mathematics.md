**INTRODUCTION**

Mathematics is the study of patterns and relationships, and it plays a vital role in developing students’ intellectual competencies such as logical reasoning, spatial visualisation, analysis, and abstract thinking. Through the learning and application of mathematical concepts, students build strong numeracy, reasoning, and problem-solving skills.

At West Grove, we strive to provide a rich variety of learning experiences that enable students to deepen their understanding of mathematical concepts and apply them meaningfully to a wide range of real-world situations.

**MISSION / VISION**

*   To provide students with concrete and meaningful learning experiences to deepen their understanding of mathematical concepts.
*   To empower students to reason mathematically and communicate their thinking clearly.
