<p><strong>Growing Persevering STEM Innovators</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Growing STEM Innovators with Perseverance</strong>
</p>
<p>At West Grove, we believe every child has the potential to shape the future.
Through our <strong>Applied Learning Programme (ALP)</strong>, we nurture
STEM Innovators — children who think critically, solve problems creatively,
and persevere even when challenges arise. Imagine your child faced with
a tricky puzzle or a real-world problem: instead of giving up, they try
again, test new ideas, and keep going until they find a solution. That
spirit of perseverance is at the heart of our ALP, helping students grow
into resilient learners who embrace challenges with confidence and curiosity.</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;Our ALP Journey</strong>
</p>
<p>Over six years, our students embark on a hands-on learning journey that
blends science, technology, engineering, and mathematics with everyday
life. Each stage builds their skills and confidence:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>P1–P2: Exploring the wonders of nature and caring for the environment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>P3–P4: Tackling sustainability challenges like recycling and food security.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>P5–P6: Innovating solutions for water and energy conservation.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Through these experiences, children learn to apply STEM thinking to real-world
issues, preparing them to be problem-solvers of tomorrow.</p>
<p>Success doesn’t come from getting everything right the first time. It
comes from trying, learning from mistakes, and trying again. That’s why
perseverance is the key learning disposition we cultivate.</p>
<p>Our students learn to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Keep going when solutions don’t come easily.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>See mistakes as opportunities to improve.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Approach challenges with resilience and creativity.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>&nbsp;Beyond the Classroom</strong>
</p>
<p>To stretch their potential, students also develop digital competencies
such as coding, computational thinking, and digital collaboration. For
those with special interests, our CCAs — Robotics, Infomedia, and Nature
Club — provide exciting opportunities to innovate further.</p>
<p>Through our ALP, we hope that our children will not only be knowledgeable
in STEM but also resilient in spirit. They will be ready to face the future
with perseverance, wisdom, and grace — true innovators who can make a positive
difference in the world.</p>
