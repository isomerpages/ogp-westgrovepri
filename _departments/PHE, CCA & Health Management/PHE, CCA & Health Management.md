</p>

<p>Know who to turn to when in need of help</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enjoy balanced meals with healthy food choices</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Engage in meaningful activities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Physically active to be fit</p>
</li>
