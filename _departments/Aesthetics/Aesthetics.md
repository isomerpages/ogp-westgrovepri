<h1>Aesthetics</h1>

</div>
<p><strong>INTRODUCTION:</strong>
</p>
<p>With the advent of the new media, increasingly, the Arts have been given
greater attention, greater importance. Indeed, Arts education is a pathway
to creativity and innovation. It opens up our imagination and unleashes
our innermost creative energies. It is also a universal language that transcends
cultural differences and geographical boundaries. As such, we aim to achieve
the following.</p>
<p><strong>MISSION:</strong>
</p>
<p>Provide an environment where students can explore, experience, and express
Arts spontaneously.</p>
<p><strong>VISION:</strong>
</p>
<p>Nurture every child to become a gracious and informed audience of the
Arts.</p>
<p><strong>In the classroom, students will:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Explore <strong>creative thinking routines</strong> that help them wonder,
question, and make connections.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learn to give and receive <strong>peer feedback</strong> in simple, constructive
ways, building confidence and collaboration.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reflect on their own growth through <strong>digital Art portfolios</strong>,
making their learning journey visible.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Take part in <strong>inquiry-based projects</strong> that encourage them
to ask questions and nurture their creativity through art and music.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Beyond the classroom, students will have the opportunity to:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Showcase their talents termly during recess</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience diverse cultures and art forms through music and art, helping
them appreciate traditions and perspectives from around the world.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Applying their skills in <strong>real-world projects</strong>—from designing
logos to composing soundscapes and writing songs.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Through these experiences, children don’t just learn techniques; they
develop resilience, creativity, and a deeper appreciation for the arts.
Most importantly, they discover the joy of learning and the power of self-expression.</p>
