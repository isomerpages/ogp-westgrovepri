# Aesthetics
![](/images/Aes_IC.png)
         

**INTRODUCTION:**

With the advent of the new media, increasingly, the Arts have been given greater attention, greater importance. Indeed, Arts education is a pathway to creativity and innovation. It opens up our imagination and unleashes our innermost creative energies. It is also a universal language that transcends cultural differences and geographical boundaries. As such, we aim to achieve the following.

**MISSION:**

Provide an environment where students can explore, experience, and express Arts spontaneously.

**VISION:**

Nurture every child to become a gracious and informed audience of the Arts.

**In the classroom, students will:**

*   Explore **creative thinking routines** that help them wonder, question, and make connections.
*   Learn to give and receive **peer feedback** in simple, constructive ways, building confidence and collaboration.
*   Reflect on their own growth through **digital Art portfolios**, making their learning journey visible.
*   Take part in **inquiry-based projects** that encourage them to ask questions and nurture their creativity through art and music.

**Beyond the classroom, students will have the opportunity to:**

*   Showcase their talents termly during recess
*   Experience diverse cultures and art forms through music and art, helping them appreciate traditions and perspectives from around the world.
*   Applying their skills in **real-world projects**—from designing logos to composing soundscapes and writing songs.

Through these experiences, children don’t just learn techniques; they develop resilience, creativity, and a deeper appreciation for the arts. Most importantly, they discover the joy of learning and the power of self-expression.
