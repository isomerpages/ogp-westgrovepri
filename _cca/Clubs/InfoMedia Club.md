<p>The mission of the club is expose our students to various mediums of digital
technology and allow them to learn &amp; explore collaboratively and at
their own pace. Through collaborations and opportunities, students will
learn, master and share their knowledge, passion and skills within and
beyond the school.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Your children will have opportunities to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Learn and practice Photography</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learn and practice coding on various platforms</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Micro: bits Block-based coding</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Scratch Block-based Coding</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>AI and machine learning</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Swift Programming Language</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>More to come...</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Share and facilitate activities for the rest of the school</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learn about various aspects of Cyber Wellness</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Share and Advocate Cyber Wellness in our school as CWSAs (Cyber Wellness
Student Ambassadors)</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
