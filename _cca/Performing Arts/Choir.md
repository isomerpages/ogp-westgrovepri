# Choir

The West Grove Chorus, set up in 2002, consists of members ranging from age 8 to 12.  Presently under the choir instructress Ms Rose Loh, the choir has, besides performing for school events, taken part on competitive platforms- Singapore Youth Festivals, community events at the Garden by the Bay and National Day community events, as well as external choir accreditation examinations â€“ London College of Music (LCM) Choir Ensemble and Singapore International Choral Festival (SICF) 2019.  

**Objectives:**

* To foster an appreciation and passion for choral singing. 
* To develop develop good singing techniques and musicality. 
* To nurture confidence in students and provide opportunities to hone showmanship skills while inculcating discipline and teamwork through performances in school events and competitions.
