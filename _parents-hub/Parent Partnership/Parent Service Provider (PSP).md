<h4><strong>Triple P – Positive Parenting Programme</strong>&nbsp;</h4>
<p>Triple P is a Positive Parenting Programme that has been successfully
conducted for parents all over the world. &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Triple_P_Individual_Consultation.jpg">
</div>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Triple P helps parents:&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Raise happy, confident children&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage misbehaviour so everyone in the family enjoys life more&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Set rules and routines that everyone respects and follows&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Encourage behaviour you like&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Take care of yourself as a parent&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Feel confident you're doing the right thing &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Five Steps to Positive Parenting:&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Creating a safe, interesting environment&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Have a positive learning environment&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use assertive discipline&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Have realistic expectations&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Take care of yourself &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ol>
